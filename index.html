What is a scrap buyer ?

A person who buys and sells old metal, etc. that can be used for new purposes.

How many types of scrap are there ?

There are two main categories of metals: ferrous and non-ferrous. Metals which contain iron in them are known as ferrous.
Metals without iron are non-ferrous. Common non-ferrous metals are copper, brass, aluminum, zinc, magnesium, tin, nickel, and lead.
